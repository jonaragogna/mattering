The Mattering Kit

Every young person needs to matter.

A clear, research-based resource for the adults who shape their lives.

The idea

Mattering is the sense that you count.

It is the felt knowledge that you are significant to someone, that your presence is real to others, that your absence would be noticed. It is close to belonging, but sharper. You can be fully included and still feel invisible.

Why it matters

When the need goes unmet, something fills the absence.

Young people who do not feel they matter look for that feeling elsewhere. Where they find it can shape the rest of their lives.

The resource

A practical way to help them matter.

The Mattering Kit turns the science of significance into concrete, usable practice for teachers, mentors, coaches, and anyone who works with young people. Free, and open to all.

Built on research

Grounded in interviews with practitioners, a preregistered study, and the established research on mattering and the human need for significance.
