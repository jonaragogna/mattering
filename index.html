SAMPLE HABITS FROM THE KIT

Give every child a small job

Hand each child one small responsibility a day. We feel we matter when others need us.

Mix the ones who never pair up

Pair children who never team up. Helping someone outside your own group builds a bond.

Greet them by name

Say every student's name at the door. Being seen is the smallest step, and it counts.

ABOUT THE KIT

Grounded in research

Built on a study of 700 people on how exclusion affects our sense of meaning, and what protects it.

Made with teachers

Shaped by interviews with teachers in the classrooms where it matters most, and reviewed by practitioners in prevention work.

Free and open

Bilingual, no sign-up, no cost. Download it today, use it tomorrow.
