SAMPLE HABITS FROM THE KIT

Greet them by name

Say each child's name as they walk in. It takes seconds and tells them they were seen.

Notice who is left out

Watch for the child on the edge of the group, and find one small way to bring them in.

Ask, then listen

Ask one child something about their day, and give them your full attention for a moment.

ABOUT THE KIT

Grounded in research

Built on a study of 700 people on how exclusion affects our sense of meaning, and what protects it.

Made with teachers

Shaped by interviews with teachers in the classrooms where it matters most, and reviewed by practitioners in prevention work.

Free and open

Bilingual, no sign-up, no cost. Download it today, use it tomorrow.
